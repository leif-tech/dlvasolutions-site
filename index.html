Agency Chatter Training

Become a Professional Agency Chatter

A structured training program for individuals who want to work as professional chatters for OnlyFans agencies and content creator management companies.

What is an agency chatter?

Agency chatters manage fan conversations on behalf of content creators. They handle subscriber engagement, build relationships, drive sales through natural dialogue, and maintain the creator's voice and persona across platforms. It's a real, in-demand role that requires training, consistency, and absolute discretion.

What You'll Learn

  • Fan engagement techniques and conversation management
  • Sales conversion and upselling through natural dialogue
  • Persona & tone matching — maintaining the creator's voice
  • AI-assisted chat workflows for speed and quality at scale
  • Strict confidentiality protocols and professional boundaries
  • Performance metrics: response time, revenue per subscriber, retention

Who it's for

  • Comfortable working in the adult content space
  • Can write naturally and adapt to different tones
  • Understand discretion is non-negotiable
  • Want structured training before touching a live account

Who it's not for

This program isn't for people who:

• Are uncomfortable with the nature of the industry
• Cannot maintain strict confidentiality
• Want shortcuts without accountability
